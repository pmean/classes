Graded: 5 points, one point deduction if submitted after the due date.

2. If you flipped a coin and chose every even numbered value in your sampling frame for heads and every odd numbered value for tails, what type of sample would you have?

3. Why can't you use a cross-over trial to compare one type of training method to another?

4. What might cause problems with a matching approach to sampling?
