### Poster structure

The information to be incorporated into the poster material generally should include:

* Title

* Background

* Methods

* Study Design

* Sample

* Measures and Materials

* Procedures

* Statistical Analysis

* Potential Implications of the Proposed Study

More detailed information concerning expectations for this presentation will be covered later in the semester. The Grading Rubric for the oral presentation is posted in the "Course Content" section of the course Canvas site.
